Command with a positional and options                       
configured using a Linq Expression, not                     
a lambda.                                                   
                                                            
exp <pos> [options]                                         
                                                            
Parameters:                                                 
                                                            
pos  A positional configured with an                        
     expression.                                            
                                                            
Options:                                                    
                                                            
**B  A boolean option configured with an                    
     expression.                                            
**I  A boolean option configured with an                    
     expression.
